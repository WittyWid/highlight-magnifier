Privacy Policy for Highlight Magnifier

Last updated: 06/29/2025

Highlight Magnifier does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data or personally identifiable information.

All processing of highlighted text happens locally within your browser. Highlight Magnifier does not send any data to external servers, and no user data is shared with third parties.

Any highlights you pin or save are stored only in your local browser storage and never transmitted outside your device.
